Pork Chops

Description

Pork chops are a common and delicious lean cut of the pig. This simple and delicious recipe makes for an easy and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 6 pork chops

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Mix the spices together in a wide, shallow bowl.
  3. Place pork chops in the spice mixture to coat and arrange on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake chops in the oven for about 20 minutes until no longer pink. The internal temperature should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
