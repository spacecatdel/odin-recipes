Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is an unique italian pasta dish the creates a creamy sauce using raw eggs, parmesan and pasta water. No cream is used! Topped with bacon, this dish is a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti to box instructions, drain and toss with olive oil.
  2. Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium heat, until evenly browned. Drain bacon on paper towels and leave 2 tablespoons of bacon fat in the skillet.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to the skillet. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add wine and cook another minute.
  4. Add the bacon and pasta to the skillet and toss. Add beaten eggs and cook, tossng constantly until eggs are barely set. Add 1/2 cup parmesan and toss again. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve warm with chopped parsley and extra parmesan cheese.
